World Happiness Report in 2021

As COVID-19 happening for years now, we study how the people around the world have fared. The aim of this report was to focus on the effects of COVID-19 on the structure and quality of people’s lives, and to describe and evaluate how governments all over the world have dealt with the pandemic.

The map on the left shows the happiness score of different countries in 2021. This reflects the capabilities of people at transitioning and adapting to the pandemic also how different governments all over the world dealt with the pandemic in relation to citizen's happiness level.

This reflects the overall happiness level in different regions by Social support, Healthy life expectancy, Freedom to make life choices, Generosity and Perceptions of corruption . It shows a solution to governments to help out their citizens during the pandemic.

We noticed that western europe dominates the chart. While Asia Pacific falls behind. In Malaysia, we are ranked at 81 while Singapore ranked 32. As a Malaysian, unarguably this is caused by the government losing control of the pandemic. However, this report shows a way for our government to improve citizens’ mental health. Also as a citizen, we do have to adapt to it since changes are not immediate.
